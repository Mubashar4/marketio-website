1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide reporting information.

2. How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies (Required)

Necessary for the platform to function. These include session management, authentication, and security tokens. You cannot opt out of these.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (Google Analytics, Hotjar). All information is aggregated and anonymous.

Marketing & Retargeting Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertising via Google Ads, LinkedIn Insight Tag, and Meta Pixel. Duration: 90 days.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences to enhance your experience.

3. Managing Your Preferences

You can manage or withdraw consent to non-essential cookies at any time by:

  • Using the Cookie Preferences centre accessible from our website footer
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Using opt-out tools provided by specific services (e.g., Google Analytics Opt-Out)

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party service providers we do not control. Please refer to their respective privacy policies: Google (policies.google.com/privacy), LinkedIn (linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy), Meta (facebook.com/privacy/policy).
